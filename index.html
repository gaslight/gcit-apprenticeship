The Apprenticeship model

  • 6 months
  • 1 experienced mentor
  • 2-4 apprentices
  • Joining a team
  • Shipping real code

Key differences

  • Not an internship/coop
  • Growing developers for you (not us)

Where do apprentices come from?

  • Boot camps
  • Self-taught

The assessment process

  • Initial interview
  • Math/Logic challenge
  • Coding together

What does an apprentice do?

  • Pair with other team members
  • Book club
  • Coding exercises
  • Week 1-1s
  • Bi-monthly assessment
  • Keep learning!
